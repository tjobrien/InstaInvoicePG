InstaInvoice

InstaInvoice is a free mobile invoicing and billing app from Breeasy.com.

When you create an InstaInvoice account on your phone or mobile device with the InstaInvoice App, an account is created in our cloud based companion web app, the Breeasy Billing, Invoicing and Time Tracking App. Conveniently, you can access the invoices you enter in the InstaInvoice app on the web, and the invoices you create on the web app are available on your mobile device. You can also apply payments, track billable hours worked, create and edit contacts, and update your account settings such as company name and address.

Please visit us at Breeasy.com for other apps built for small business by a small business.
